An Interview With A Luxury Traveller: "The Maasai Mara is the greatest place on the planet."

If you’re talking about pictures that speak vibrance, positive energy, and adventure, head to @justintensitysworld on Instagram for an amazing time scrolling through the world.

Justin is a blogger, luxury traveller, adventurer & lifestyle influencer on social media. Granted the opportunity to have a chat with him, here are his answers to a couple of our questions.

Where was the first place you travelled that made you feel - wow travel is amazing?

I got the travel bug pretty early in life as a kid travelling with my family. The special place that had me in awe is the Maasai Mara in Kenya — the single greatest place to go on a Safari in the world. It remains my favourite place on planet earth and I’ve been on Safari there probably thirty plus times now.

If you had unlimited resources, what would you do and where would you go?

I would probably still be a lawyer because I love my job, but I’d run my own firm and keep my own schedule— that way, I could incorporate a lot more travel into my annual schedule. My number one priority would be to travel to as many African countries as possible, starting with Mauritius.

What inspired you to travel for an extended period of time or live in a new country?

I’ve twice travelled for an extended period. I’m currently 8 months into a 12-month Hawaii stay. Generally, I’m a big believer in acquiring knowledge through experience. I’m a student of the world and I love learning about different cultures. I think it fosters tolerance, understanding, and togetherness.

I travel because…

I want to learn as much as I can about all the different and amazing things the world has to offer.
