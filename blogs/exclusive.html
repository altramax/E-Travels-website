Exclusive Interview With Instagram Travel & Lifestyle Personality MARINA P.

Marina P. also known as @marinkeys on Instagram is a radiant and young individual originating from Ukraine. She’s been attracting attention on social media through her mind-blowing photos of mother nature and the uniqueness in her style of photography. Her photos and videos are vibrant, significant and capture some of nature’s most amazing phenomena.

In our discussion with her, she explains little about her passion for traveling, the challenges involved and shares some insights on her journey. Learn about the woman behind the amazing travel & lifestyle photography of @marinkeys in our exclusive interview below.

What do you do?

I am on my way to discover inner peace and serenity.

When and how did you develop an interest in travel?

Since childhood, I think. I was always interested in our planet and read a lot about countries, marine life, and wild animals.

What are you most passionate about when you travel?

The ultimate freedom to find new adventures. I always prefer to come to places with unimaginable nature. Sometimes I can’t believe that this beauty is real. I like to speak to local people. They always know some cool stories and can tell about their everyday life. And animals…! I was kissed by a cheetah on Zanzibar, fed a white lion, lived on a ranch, and saw hundreds of dolphins in the Atlantic. I have just returned from Maldives where I was accidentally lucky to swim with the whale shark. On this trip, I spent a huge amount of time underwater.
