The Nigerian education system is in severe crisis. It’s one of the countries with poor education systems in Africa and the world. This poor standard of education leaves much to be desired. Furthermore, it compels young ones who pursue quality education to constantly seek educational opportunities outside the country. Sadly, they have a challenge—Financing! Yes, most foreign institutions are expensive, limiting students from actualizing their goals.

Are you seeking to obtain quality education outside Nigeria but do not have the resources to fund your schooling? MuffyTravels has got you covered! We help students gain admission into top-tier institutions outside the country without breaking the bank!

Countries Available;

  1. Canada

    Canada delivers outstanding quality of education, has some of the affordable tuition fees in the world, offers graduates with a strong earning potential, and is recognized as one of the safest nations in the world for foreign students. Canada is home to 10 of the leading 250 universities worldwide and has 4 of the top 100 best student cities worldwide. In Canada, you will learn with the best and experience a culture, climate, and way of life beyond comparison.

  2. The United Kingdom

    The United Kingdom is the second most famous destination for overseas students. A degree from affordable United Kingdom universities will look great on a CV and when searching for a job in the future, irrespective of the industry and location. A qualification from one of the renowned universities in the UK is an excellent selling point. Uk universities are highly esteemed, with experts on several academic topics. As a multinational company, international students can enjoy diversity, from food, drinks, arts, etc. While there are brilliant work opportunities for foreign students, they can also enjoy some financial benefits, making it easy to pay tuition fees.

  3. The United States

    The United States higher education system is one of the world-best - for teaching methods and academic standards! The US has over 350 universities featured in the 2021 QS World University Rankings and has five universities in the leading 10 QS World University Rankings 2021. A degree from one of the renowned and affordable institutions in the US will make a graduate stand out to potential employers. While studying in the United States, foreign students can take advantage of internships to obtain real-world experience. International students have the rare privilege to access world-class support systems for academics and learning. Foreign students in the United States enjoy cultural diversities and have the opportunity to study, work, and socialize with people from varying cultures and backgrounds. Such beautiful learning environment help students experience a vibrant lifestyle.

  4. Ireland

    Ireland is an excellent destination for foreign students looking for a world-class education. Renowned universities in Ireland make it to the leading higher universities globally. International students are open to a vast array of scholarships, and Ireland is a safe, welcoming, and friendly country, ranking 12th on the Global Peace Index. As a progressive, innovative nation with promising career opportunities, international students can anticipate the best experience in Ireland. International students will find scenery, mountain walks, architecture, and ancient culture stunning and alluring. Stay-back visa option available to international students in Ireland is another excellent choice to study in one of their universities.

  5. Australia

    Currently, Australia is the third most prominent destination for foreign students in English-speaking countries. The high quality of education, laid-back nature, and friendly natives are reasons foreign students consider Australia for study. The Australian education system is carefully and constantly regulated by the government to retain the high standards of education the country is famous for. Living expenses and tuition fees in Australia are lower. International students can work part-time as they study, helping them offset their living costs. Amazingly, you are open to mouth-watering, which would lower your study expenses. While studying in Australia, foreign students can work for 20 hours each week, an incredible opportunity to earn, offset their bills and gain working experience.
